About PetRanker

PetRanker is a mobile application designed for pet owners to share photos of their pets, participate in friendly competitions, and engage with a community of pet enthusiasts. Our platform provides a safe, engaging environment for showcasing your beloved pets and discovering other amazing pets in the community.

App Functionality

  • Photo Sharing: Upload and share photos of your pets securely
  • Competition System: Participate in head-to-head pet photo competitions
  • Voting Mechanism: Vote for your favorite pets in various matchups
  • Profile Management: Create and manage your pet's profile
  • Leaderboards: Track rankings and performance statistics
  • Social Features: Connect with other pet owners and enthusiasts

Data Usage Transparency

We collect and use the following data to provide our services:

  • Account Information: Email address and basic profile details for account creation and management
  • Authentication Data: Information from social login providers (Google, Facebook, Apple) when you choose to use these services
  • User Content: Pet photos and descriptions that you choose to share
  • Usage Data: Basic interaction data to improve the app experience

All data collection is done with user consent and in accordance with our privacy policy. We prioritize user privacy and data security in all our operations.
